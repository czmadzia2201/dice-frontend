📖 About the Game

This application is based on the classic dice game Yahtzee. The goal is to achieve the highest possible score by assigning each roll to one of thirteen scoring categories.

Classic Rules

A game consists of 13 rolls. Each roll contains five dice. Every roll must be assigned to exactly one category, and each category can be used only once.

Scoring Categories

Category Description Example Score
Ones Sum of all dice showing 1 1, 1, 4, 5, 6 Sum of matching dice. Example score: 2
Twos Sum of all dice showing 2 2, 2, 3, 5, 6 Sum of matching dice. Example score: 4
Threes Sum of all dice showing 3 3, 3, 3, 2, 6 Sum of matching dice. Example score: 9
Fours Sum of all dice showing 4 4, 4, 2, 5, 6 Sum of matching dice. Example score: 8
Fives Sum of all dice showing 5 5, 5, 1, 2, 6 Sum of matching dice. Example score: 10
Sixes Sum of all dice showing 6 6, 6, 6, 2, 1 Sum of matching dice. Example score: 18
Three of a Kind At least three identical dice 4, 4, 4, 2, 6 Sum of all dice
Four of a Kind At least four identical dice 5, 5, 5, 5, 2 Sum of all dice
Full House Three identical dice and two other identical dice 2, 2, 2, 5, 5 25
Small Straight Four consecutive values 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 30
Large Straight Five consecutive values 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 40
Poker (Yahtzee) Five identical dice 6, 6, 6, 6, 6 50
Chance Any combination of dice 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 Sum of all dice

The order of the dice does not matter. For example, a Large Straight can be scored with 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 as well as 5, 3, 2, 1, 4.

Additional Features

This application has been extended with additional features to enhance your gameplay experience.

Manual Best

The Manual Best mode lets you create your own alternative assignment of rolls to categories after the game is finished and all rolls are known. Try to find a better or even optimal assignment of categories.

Optimal Score

Finally, you can also compare your own solutions with the optimal one. The Optimal Score feature analyzes all rolls played in the game and calculates the highest score that could have been achieved by assigning rolls to categories in the best possible way.

Using these features is entirely optional, but they can add an extra layer of challenge and analysis to the game.
